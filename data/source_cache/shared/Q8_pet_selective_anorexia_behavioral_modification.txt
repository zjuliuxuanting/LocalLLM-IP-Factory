**核心发现：**
A fussy eater dog can test your patience and worry your heart. You want to feed your pet well. You want to see them eat with joy. When a picky eater dog turns away, it's stressful for both of you. The good news is that fussy eating in dogs is common and has solutions.

**Health Issues vs Behavioral Reasons（健康问题与行为原因对比）：**
- **Health Issues That Can Reduce Your Dog's Appetite**：Medical issues are common reasons for a dog's loss of appetite. Some are minor, while others need quick vet care. Stomach upset (vomiting and diarrhea), Gut dysbiosis (bacterial imbalance in the digestive system causes inflammation), Dental problems (sore gums, cavities, ulcers, or mouth injuries make chewing painful)
- **Behavioral Reasons Behind Selective Eating**：Some dogs learn that refusing food leads to better options. This creates a cycle of partial anorexia where the dog holds out for something more appealing

**Breaking the Partial Anorexia Cycle（打破选择性厌食循环）：**
Recognizing Overfeeding and Treat Dependency：Many fussy eaters are actually just overfed or treat-dependent. The dog has learned that if they wait long enough, a more palatable option will appear. Establishing Boundaries Around Table Scraps is critical to breaking this pattern

**Food Aversion and Neophobia（食物厌恶与新食物恐惧）：**
When毛孩身体不適（如化療後的噁心感、腸胃發炎）時，若強行灌食，牠會將「食物」與「痛苦/恐懼」產生強烈連結，形成食物厭惡（Food Aversion）。

**Positive Reinforcement Techniques（正向强化技术）：**
The method pairs the act of approaching, sniffing, or ingesting the kibble with a highly valued consequence, such as a preferred treat, verbal praise, or brief play. Consistency in timing ensures the animal links the specific behavior with the reward, accelerating learning.

**Implementing the Technique（实施步骤）：**
1. **Identify a primary reinforcer** that the dog values more than the dry food; this may be a soft morsel, a favorite toy, or enthusiastic verbal affirmation
2. **Present the kibble** in a controlled environment with minimal distractions
3. **Reward immediately** when the dog approaches or sniffs the food
4. **Gradually increase the requirement** from sniffing to eating

**Feeding Environment Optimization（喂食环境优化）：**
Creating a Positive Feeding Environment is essential for dogs with food refusal issues. Establishing a Routine helps reduce anxiety and creates predictability. Avoiding Free-Feeding prevents the dog from learning that they can always wait for something better
