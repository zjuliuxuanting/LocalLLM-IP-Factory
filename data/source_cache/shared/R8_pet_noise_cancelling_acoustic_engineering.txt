本文对主流宠物降噪耳机产品进行了全面评测，包括被动隔音和主动降噪两种技术路线。

**Passive vs Active Noise Cancellation（被动与主动降噪对比）：**
- **Passive Noise Cancellation**：Relies on physical barriers such as padded ear cups or compression bands to muffle loud noises. Products like Happy Hoodie use a tight-fitting fabric band that wraps around the dog's head, covering the ears and reducing sound intensity through material absorption
- **Active Noise Cancellation (ANC)**：Uses electronic components to generate anti-noise signals that cancel out incoming sound waves. PAWNIX represents the leading ANC technology for pets

**Noise Reduction Rating (NRR)（噪声降低等级）：**
I primarily advise reviewing the Noise Reduction Rating (NRR) expressed in decibels (dB), which indicates the product's tested capability to lower noise exposure. Additionally, assess the material composition of the internal padding and the adjustability of the head strap
