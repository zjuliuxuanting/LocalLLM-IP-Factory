# Rico边境牧羊犬：词汇召回的极限

> Source: https://www.science.org/doi/abs/10.1126/science.aaf3777

## 核心研究

Hans (Border Collie), "Border collies understand hundreds of words" — 早期词汇量研究。Kaminski et al., "Word learning in a domestic dog: evidence for' fast mapping'" — 快速映射能力。Dror et al., "Dogs with a vocabulary of object labels retain them for at least 2 years" — 长期记忆保留。

## 实验发现

Rico 是首批被证明能够理解数百个物体名称的狗之一，能够在召回任务中准确识别并获取指定的物品。
